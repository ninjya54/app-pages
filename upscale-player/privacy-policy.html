1. Introduction

UpscalePlayer ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our video player application.

Key Point

UpscalePlayer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All video processing is performed locally on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

UpscalePlayer is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, or any identifying information
  • Video Content: Your videos are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere
  • Usage Analytics: We do not track how you use the app
  • Location Data: We do not access your location
  • Device Identifiers: We do not collect device IDs or advertising identifiers
  • Browsing History: We do not track your activity

3. Local Processing

All video upscaling and enhancement processing occurs entirely on your device:

  • Videos are processed using your device's GPU (Metal framework)
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Processed videos remain on your device

4. File Access

UpscalePlayer requires access to your device's files to:

  • Read video files you choose to play
  • Access your photo library (if you grant permission) to play videos stored there

This access is only used to load videos for playback. We do not copy, upload, or share your files.

5. Third-Party Services

UpscalePlayer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

In-App Purchases

If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles your data.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being compromised through our app. Your videos and settings remain secure on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

UpscalePlayer does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: support@antigravity.dev
